ABOUT US

Welcome to our delectable Cake Palace! Our webpage is designed to indulge your senses in a world of sweet, irresistible treats that are guaranteed to make your taste buds dance with delight. From classic flavors to innovative creations, our cake website is your one-stop destination for all things cake-related. Let's take a virtual tour of what you can expect to find on our enticing webpage.


The cake Palace started as a small family bakery in Matara, Walgama over 30 years ago. Since then our horizons have grown but we have never lost track of our craft heritage. Each day in-store we hand-make an extensive range of confectionary and savoury food. We make everything from the traditional custard cream slice and chocolate muse to Coffee and Chocolate gateaux's. We are experts in occasion cakes and confectionary providing everything from bespoke wedding cakes to creative confectionary with corporate logos. We make them and sell them, so we can be sure to give both quality and value.
